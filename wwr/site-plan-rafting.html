Purpose

Imagine that you are the owner of the rafting business and have decided that it is time for you to have an online presence. What is the purpose? You might need to do a little research if you are unfamiliar with water rafting companies.

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website primarily consists of adventure enthusiasts, outdoor enthusiasts, and thrill-seekers who are seeking an exhilarating experience on the water.

Who is likely to visit your site?

Our site is likely to attract individuals who enjoy outdoor activities such as water rafting, kayaking, and other adventure sports. They may range from beginners looking for a new experience to experienced rafters seeking challenging rapids.

What are their demographics?

Target audience typically includes individuals aged 18-45, both men and women, who are physically active and enjoy outdoor recreational activities. They may come from diverse backgrounds and occupations, but share a passion for adventure.

What are their interests and preferences?

Our audience is interested in adrenaline-pumping experiences, nature exploration, and outdoor adventures. They prefer activities that offer a mix of excitement, physical challenge, and natural beauty.
